Fine-Tuning With
LoRA — Less is More

Low-Rank Adaptation inserts tiny trainable matrices into Whisper's attention layers. The rest of the model stays frozen. The result: full adaptation at a fraction of the cost.

196,608 trainable parameters out of 72 million total — that's 0.27%
8-bit quantization halves GPU memory — trainable on Apple Silicon
SpecAugment (frequency + time masking) reduces overfitting by 20–30% WER
Effective batch size 16 via gradient accumulation (4 steps × 4 samples)
