What this research proves — and why it matters beyond the technical results
01
Language Inclusion Is an AI Justice Issue
220M+ Nigerians deserve AI that can hear them. Building for under-represented languages is not a niche academic exercise — it is foundational to what intelligence means in a global context.
02
LoRA Democratises AI Research
Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning lets a single researcher adapt a 244M-parameter model on a laptop. You don't need a data centre to do meaningful, world-class AI research in 2026.
03
Data Is the Bottleneck — Community Is the Solution
No training technique compensates for missing data. The crowdsourcing platform at ngs.primeeralabs.com is the path to the scale of data these languages need.
04
The Work Is Not Done — But the Path Is Clear
More data, dialect-aware evaluation, mobile deployment, and JustTalk integration are the next milestones. The trajectory is set. What's needed now is more voices joining the effort.
