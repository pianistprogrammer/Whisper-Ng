Why Community Sourcing Is the Only Path
Google and Mozilla have made significant contributions, but Nigerian voices in those datasets are still a fraction of what's needed. Academic datasets lack the dialectal range and conversational naturalness of real speech. Only a platform designed for and by Nigerian speakers can produce the data that will make AI truly inclusive.
